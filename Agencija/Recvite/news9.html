Candidate Recruitin Experience: The Importance

  • Home
  • News and Times
  • CDL Hire, Recruiment, Tips

The way candidates experience recruiting is an important thing. Treating your candidate well, combined with the employer’s reputation leads to successful employer branding. As a result, this will improve the overall process and quality of hires.

Candidate experience is a candidate’s overall perception of the company’s recruiting process.

Most job candidates agree that the way they’re treated in the recruiting process is a clear indicator of how a company will treat them at work. It incorporates all the feelings, behaviors and attitudes candidates experience during the process of recruitment. Meaning every interaction a candidate has with the recruiter affects his/her experience. As a result, having a positive candidate experience isn’t just another trend, it is a necessity! Meaning a bad experience in recruiting can have a disastrous effect in the end.

4 tips to improve the candidate experience:

1. Be honest about the position. Don’t tell your candidate that he will be home for 5 days after 3 weeks on the road if you will offer him only 3 days off.
2. Transparent recruiting process;
3. Easy application process. By using recruitment software such as EagleRMS you can have a simple application process through a simple form.
4. Maintain communication with candidates.

Why are YOU failing?

1. Are you thinking that bad candidate experience has no effect on your company? You’re wrong. Employer review sites can and will affect your business;
2. Set clear expectations; give and ask for feedback;
3. Implement new AI for sourcing, screening, and MORE.
